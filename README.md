## 📝 What is LetterforTwin?
Texting is fast, but it can feel a bit empty. We send hundreds of messages a day, but they often get lost in the noise of group chats and notifications. 

I built **LetterforTwin** to fix that. It’s a quiet, ad-free space where you can take your time to write a real letter. No blue bubbles, no "typing..." indicators. Just a beautiful, private place to write something meaningful. 

## ✨ Cool Features I Built

I wanted the app to feel like you are handling physical paper. Here is what it can do:

*   🎨 **Design Your Own Covers:** Before they even read the letter, they see the envelope. You can add photos, change colors, and add a 3D wax seal that they actually have to "break" to open the letter.
*   📖 **3D Page Flipping:** When reading a letter, the pages actually flip in 3D like a real book.
*   ✒️ **Draw Your Signature:** Using a digital pen tool, you can draw your actual signature at the bottom of the page using your mouse or finger.
*   🎙️ **Voice Notes & Photos:** You aren't limited to just text. You can record your voice or attach photos right onto the pages.
*   🔥 **Burn After Reading:** Send a secret. If you turn this on, the letter will permanently delete itself forever as soon as the person finishes reading it and closes the app.
*   ⏳ **Time Capsules:** Write a letter today, but lock it so your friend can't open it until a specific date in the future (like a birthday or anniversary).
*   💾 **Never Lose Your Work:** The app auto-saves as you type. Even if your internet goes out, your draft is saved safely on your device.

## 🛠️ How I Built It (Tech Stack)

Even though the app looks simple and clean on the outside, there is a lot going on behind the scenes!

*   **Frontend:** HTML5, CSS3, and Vanilla JavaScript.
*   **Animations:** [GSAP](https://greensock.com/gsap/) (This is what makes the 3D book flip and the wax seal pop).
*   **Backend & Login:** Firebase (Using Google Login and a real-time database to send the letters instantly).
*   **Media Storage:** Cloudinary (To safely store the photos and voice recordings).
*   **Local Storage:** IndexedDB (To handle the offline auto-saving).
